Garlic rice

Garlic rice is a type of Filipino rice where you fry garlic along with the rice. You need to add salt and seasoning to make it better.

How to cook

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Seasoning
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Put oil
  2. Fry garlic for 2 mins
  3. Mix rice
  4. Add salt and seasoning
