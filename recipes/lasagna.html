Lasagna

Lasagna for me is the best pasta. The thick coating of pasta, melting cheese, and the soft beef makes it number one.

How to cook

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Boil ground beef for 30 mins
  3. Boil Pasta for 10 mins
  4. Add cheese
